# SUNKEN CRYPT — Zone Art Direction

## Zone Identity
The first descent. Ancient flooded halls where the boundary between life and death has dissolved entirely.

## Palette
Near-black, abyssal dark blue, bone-pale flesh, amber gold, blood-rust red

## Lighting
Single amber torch from above — warm gold light raking down from the top of frame. The light does not reach the water. Cold abyssal dark blue below. Caustic water reflections ripple across every surface — undulating amber lines scattered by black water movement. The creature occupies the zone between warm and cold: torchlit above the waterline, lost in dark below.

## Style Modifiers
Every creature is wet. Water clings to hair, cloth, bone, skin. Waterlogged. Bloated. Slick. Light refracts on wet surfaces — specular amber highlights on pale flesh. Bubbles rise in the dark water behind the subject. The horror here is what water does to bodies over time, and what happens when those bodies refuse to stay still.

## Portrait Specs
Waist-up or full-body centered, vertical portrait 1024x1024. Subject fills the frame. Background is zone atmosphere only — abyssal dark water, rising bubbles, caustic torch reflection on the surface. No environmental detail.
