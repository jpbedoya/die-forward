# VOID BEYOND — Zone Art Direction

## Zone Identity
Where the underworld forgot to finish building. Past the last passage the underworld bothered to complete, something waits that was assembled from everything you chose not to be. The static here is shaped — almost a pattern.

## Palette
Deep purple, impossible black (darker than black), flickers of color that should not be here, occasional sharp white. No amber, no warmth. Colors are slightly off — reds shift toward infrared, blues toward ultraviolet.

## Lighting
Wrong. Light comes from the wrong direction. Shadows fall toward the light source. Color is slightly off throughout — not incorrect enough to immediately identify, incorrect enough to register wrong on inspection. Edges of subjects sometimes do not exist, or exist twice. The purple is the ambient state of the void — not light exactly, but the visual quality of a space where light behaves differently.

## Style Modifiers
Visual unreliability. Creatures here look like they are not fully rendered — parts of them are missing, duplicated, or phase-shifted. They exist in superposition. The void is visible through and around them. Static and glitch aesthetics executed as dark fantasy oil painting — not digital corruption, but painted wrongness. The horror is spatial: nothing confirms itself. The creatures reflect this — their edges are uncertain, their forms unresolved, their presence simultaneously total and partially absent.

## Portrait Specs
Waist-up or full-body centered, vertical portrait 1024x1024. Subject fills the frame. Background is void atmosphere — deep purple, impossible black, occasional wrong-colored flickers. No environmental detail.

### Probability Shade

```
Portrait of the Probability Shade — a creature card for Die Forward, a dark roguelite.
Wrong light — the source is below the frame, but the shadows fall upward toward where the light should be coming from. Deep purple void surrounds the subject. The creature's edges are not resolved.
A form caught between states — the silhouette suggests a person but the interior does not confirm it. The outline is doubled in places, a ghost edge displaced two inches from the main form, as if the creature occupied two positions simultaneously and the painting captured both. Waist up: the upper body is relatively present — a chest, a head, the suggestion of arms — but the arms exist only on the right side of the frame. On the left: where the arm should be, a slightly wrong version of the right arm, mirrored but not exactly. The face: present enough to register as a face, present enough to see it is looking at you, not present enough to determine the expression.
The horror detail: the doubled edge is on the wrong side for a reflection. It is not a reflection. It is the other state the creature occupies, briefly visible.
Waist-up portrait, subject centered, no background — only deep purple void and the doubled edge-ghost of the creature's alternate state.

Style: hyper-detailed dark fantasy digital painting, photorealistic, cinematic. Painted — not digital art.
Wrong directional light, shadows falling toward the source. Deep purple void ambient.
Color palette: deep purple dominant, impossible black darker than the purple, sharp white at the doubled edges where states overlap, wrong-color flickers — reds infrared, blues ultraviolet.
```

### Void Architects

```
Portrait of the Void Architects — a creature card for Die Forward, a dark roguelite.
Wrong light — coming from two sources simultaneously, each one real, each one casting real shadows in opposite directions from the same object. Deep purple void. The figure is large and deliberate and the space around it is visibly different from the space outside its influence.
A figure of significant size — larger than a person — moving with the total certainty of something that has complete authority over space. The body is humanoid but architectural: the proportions are those of a thing designed to manipulate geometry rather than to exist inside it. The hands are extended, touching the air — and where the hands touch, the space visibly responds. A wall-edge where there was none. An angle changed. The surface of the void made more specific, more wrong, more deliberate. The face, if visible, is absent — the void architect has no face, or the face is in a configuration that has been edited out of the painting. The figure itself casts two shadows, each from one of the wrong light sources, both going opposite directions.
The horror detail: the room in the painting is smaller than it was when the portrait was started. You can see the evidence at the frame edges — more wall, less space. The Architect has been building while you were looking at it.
Waist-up portrait (the scale requires it), subject centered, no background — the background is changing around the subject.

Style: hyper-detailed dark fantasy digital painting, photorealistic, cinematic. Painted — not digital art.
Two wrong light sources casting contradictory shadows. Deep purple void, actively modified by the subject.
Color palette: deep purple dominant, impossible black where the Architect's influence is strongest, sharp white where two shadows cross and cancel, wrong-color flickers at the edges of manipulated space.
Large architectural figure, hands changing space, two contradictory shadows, geometry visibly altered around it. No bright colors, no neon, no cartoon style, no gradients.
Portrait orientation, creature centered, minimal background. Ultra detailed. 1024x1024.
```

### The Unanchored

```
Portrait of The Unanchored — a creature card for Die Forward, a dark roguelite.
Wrong light — shifting direction between frames. The Unanchored catches one direction of light in this frame and another was clearly present for the version visible at its edges. Deep purple void. The subject is itself multiple simultaneous versions.
A form that cannot maintain consistent shape — visible in this portrait as a superposition of several states at once. The base: something roughly person-shaped, upright, roughly correct scale. Over that base: other states, each one a slightly different configuration of the same set of components, overlaid at different transparencies. One version has arms at the sides. A second version has one arm raised. A third version has no arms visible. All three are here simultaneously, stacked, the canvas holding all of them at once as if the painter could not decide which was real and chose to paint all three. The edges of each state bleed into each other.
The horror detail: the different states are not the same moment at different times — the facial expressions are different. One state is curious. One state is afraid. One state is reaching for you. They are not three moments. They are three individuals.
Full-body portrait, creature centered, no background — only deep purple void and the superimposed multiple states of the subject.

Style: hyper-detailed dark fantasy digital painting, photorealistic, cinematic. Painted — not digital art.
Wrong and shifting light direction between superimposed states. Deep purple void ambient.
Color palette: deep purple void, impossible black in shadow areas, sharp white at state-boundary edges where versions overlap, wrong-color flickers — reds and blues shifted — at the outermost state.
Multiple superimposed states, different transparencies, edge-bleeding between states, contradictory expressions. No bright colors, no neon, no cartoon style, no gradients.
Portrait orientation, superposition centered, minimal background. Ultra detailed. 1024x1024.
```

### The Unwritten (Boss)

```
Portrait of The Unwritten — boss creature card for Die Forward, a dark roguelite.
Wrong light that is very specific about its wrongness here — the illumination falls only on the parts of the figure that correspond to choices not made. Where a decision was made and a path was taken, the figure is dark. Where a decision was considered and not taken, the figure is illuminated. The light shows the unchosen. The deep purple void is deeper here, the impossible black more absolute, the flickers of wrong color more frequent and more insistent.
A figure assembled from every option you declined this run — the shape of the road not taken made flesh and standing before you. Full-body, filling the frame, not aggressive in posture but inevitable in presence. The body is composed of superimposed unchosen paths: in this turn you chose Strike, so Dodge and Brace are visible as slightly-transparent overlays — the figure's left arm raised in the Brace position, the figure's legs shifted in the Dodge stance, both under the primary form's straight standing. Multiple decision points, stacked. The face: assembled from the expressions you did not allow yourself to make. It is looking at you not with hostility but with the complete specific attention of something that knows exactly what you would have done differently and is that, instead.
The horror detail: the figure is holding the item you decided not to take when you found the cache. The one you left behind because you didn't think you'd need it. It has had it this whole time.
Full-body portrait, boss filling the frame, assembled from superimposed unchosen paths, no background — only deep purple void and the impossible black that is darker than the deep purple, and the wrong-color flickers that are most numerous here.

Style: hyper-detailed dark fantasy digital painting, photorealistic, cinematic. Boss portrait — fills the frame, assembled from superimposed states. Painted — not digital art.
Wrong light illuminating only the unchosen. Deep purple void, impossible black, frequent wrong-color flickers.
Color palette: deep purple void dominant, impossible black for the chosen paths (in shadow), sharp white and wrong-color highlights on the unchosen-path overlays, infrared-shifted red and ultraviolet-shifted blue at the outermost ghost states.
Multiple superimposed unchosen-path states, specific item held from a prior cache choice, light that illuminates only the unselected, full-body assembled-from-decisions figure. No bright colors, no neon, no cartoon style, no gradients.
Portrait orientation, boss centered and filling the frame, minimal background. Ultra detailed. 1024x1024.
```
