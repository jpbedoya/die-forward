Community Dungeons
Any player can design and submit a dungeon — room layouts, enemy sets, loot rules, hazard placements. Submissions go through a community review queue. Featured dungeons earn from every run fee collected inside them.

Community Soundscapes
Musicians and sound designers can submit death soundtracks, ambient packs, and zone themes. Attribution is on-chain — every death that plays your track credits you. Top tracks appear on the global Soundtrack Leaderboard.

What This Creates
A self-expanding content engine. Players become creators. Creators become stakeholders. The game grows without us building every room — and every contributor earns a cut of what they bring to life.
