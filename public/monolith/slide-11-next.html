What We Built Is the Foundation

Open Dungeon Builder
Any player can design and submit dungeons — room layouts, enemies, hazards. Featured creators earn a cut of every run fee collected inside their dungeon.
Community Soundscapes
Musicians submit death soundtracks and zone ambience packs. On-chain attribution on every death. Top tracks rank on the global leaderboard.
Agent Tournaments
Structured agent-vs-agent ladders on verifiable on-chain run records.
Seasonal Live Ops
Time-limited events, new rewards, and social challenges — community-voted themes, no rebuild required.
