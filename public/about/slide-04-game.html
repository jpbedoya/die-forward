THE CORE LOOP · 5–10 MIN RUNS
STAKE
Offer something you fear losing — nothing, earned coins, or SOL. The stake is tension, not yield.
DESCEND
Route through a branching map whose doors moved overnight. Side chambers, item-gated doors, risk-tagged choices.
FIGHT
Strike, Dodge, Brace, Flee, Bait — every creature has a signature behavior. Reading the creature is the skill.
DIE — OR ESCAPE
Escape returns your stake with a bonus. Death takes it — and writes a verifiable on-chain record either way.
YOUR DEATH FEEDS THE WORLD
Your corpse, final words, and death-song become content other players discover — and raw material for tomorrow's shift.
