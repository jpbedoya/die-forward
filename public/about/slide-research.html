WHY THIS SHAPE — THE RESEARCH
Every Design Choice Has a Precedent
Roguelite money lives in East Asia
Archero 2's #1 market: South Korea, 23% of spend; Survivor.io takes 75%+ of revenue from TW/CN/KR; Japan's ARPU runs ~4× global. That's why Die Forward ships fully localized in 7 languages — JP, KR and Traditional Chinese first.
Death-as-content is a proven playbook
NetHack bones files, Dark Souls bloodstains and curse statues, Shiren's rescue system — 30 years of async-death design, native to Japan. The Shift's apex threats, curse statues, and Architect walls lift directly from this lineage.
Web3 gaming's one working playbook
93% of GameFi projects are dead; ~$15B burned. The survivor pattern is game-first (Off The Grid). So: stake as thrill, never yield, no token, free game excellent standalone, chain revealed progressively.
TIER A — JP · KR · TW (revenue)
TIER B — US · EU (identity & discovery)
TIER C — VN · PH · BR (crypto-native scale)
The fiction is the moat — Archero has the loop but no soul; Dark Souls has the mystique but no mobile presence; web3 had stakes but no game.
